Bellwether Education Finance Equity Training, Fall 2022

This page will be your primary source for class lectures, instructions, and sample data.

This hands-on training covers two tracks of content:

  • Building interactive tools to illustrate school finance data and model policy options.
  • How to leverage interactive tools in coalition and advocacy work.
